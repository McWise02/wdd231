Our Mission

At Gold and Myrrh Games, we create uplifting and faith-centered video games that help individuals grow spiritually while strengthening family bonds. We believe games can be both fun and deeply meaningful, serving as tools for connection, learning, and joy.

Meet the Team

Our Story

Founded in 2024, Gold and Myrrh Games started with a small group of LDS game developers who wanted to build more than entertainment—they wanted to build testimonies. What began as a side project has now become a growing movement, with games that encourage scripture study, teamwork, and Christlike values.
