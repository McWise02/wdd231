Our Mission

At Gold and Myrrh, our mission is to create inspiring, faith-based video games that strengthen testimonies, bring families closer together, and help Latter-day Saints of all ages grow in their love for the gospel. Through meaningful storytelling and uplifting gameplay, we aim to make faith fun, interactive, and part of everyday life.
